## **Professional Summary**

Jitendra "Jitu" Raut is a Product Designer, Framer Expert, Entrepreneur, and Community Builder with more than 12 years of experience designing digital products across fintech, e-commerce, SaaS, enterprise software, Web3, and no-code ecosystems.

Throughout his career, Jitu has worked with some of the most recognized technology companies and startups including Flipkart, CheQ, GK Software Germany, CoutLoot, Workmeet, and various international consulting projects across London, Berlin, and Cairo.

He specializes in Product Design, User Experience Design, Design Systems, Motion Design, Prototyping, and No-Code Development. Over the years he has evolved from a UI Designer into a Design Leader, leading teams, building design systems, mentoring designers, and creating products used by millions of users.

Today, Jitu runs Windesign Labs OPC Private Limited, a design and no-code studio focused on Framer website development, premium Framer templates, product design consulting, and AI-powered digital experiences.
